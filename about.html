About

We at ArtBargain believe that to succeed in commerce you must be grounded in culture. That is to say there is no shop without the customers, and what they bring is as important as the art works we source from artists from all around the world.

We have been active on 'The scene' for weeks, with a shop on the high street and a web site that can be viewed on both mobiles and laptops, with no appreciable difference in the content and features presented to the peruser. Our inventory of items is increasing all the time and our customer base have nothing but good things to say about us.

Likewise the artists whose works we offer for sale tell us that we are a reliable partner in the monetisation of their pieces.
